Our Heritage

Our Brand

Uma Kodiveri Athi was founded with a simple mission—to bring the authentic taste of Original Kodiveri Athipalam directly from nature to every home.

Located near the beautiful Kodiveri Dam, Erode, our fruits are carefully harvested from naturally grown trees and selected for their freshness, quality, and rich nutritional value. Along with fresh Athipalam, we proudly offer a range of traditional natural products including Dry Figs, Fig Jam, Fig Powder, Wild Honey, Mudavattu Kilangu, and One Head Garlic.

Our Leadership

Our Founder

Uma Kodiveri Athi is proudly led by Mrs. K. Uma Maheswari, whose passion for natural farming and traditional foods inspired the creation of this brand.

With years of experience in sourcing authentic Kodiveri Athipalam, she believes that nature provides the best nourishment when preserved in its purest form. Every product is personally selected with a strong focus on quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction, ensuring that each family receives authentic products directly from Kodiveri.

"To make Original Kodiveri Athipalam and traditional natural products available to every home while preserving their authenticity, purity, and health benefits."
